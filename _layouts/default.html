About

Hey there! I’m Noah, an undergraduate at UC Berkeley double-majoring in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics (Data Science cluster). I love almost anything numerical, as well as learning, teaching, and developing relationships with people. I’ve worked on both individual and team projects related to programming, robotics, and music, and look to expand my knowledge whenever I can! I have an eye for improvement, and love to solve problems with others.

On the side, my passion for passing down knowledge persists. I am a member of Pioneers in Engineering, a club that strives to provide affordable robotics competitions to high school students. I play piano, guitar, and sing, and am a co-president of CalCommunityMusic, an organization dedicated to performing for senior homes in Berkeley. I am also a member of the UC Berkeley Chess Club.

Currently, I'm working on a voice-controlled car, using systems and control to navigate the physical world. I'm also in the process of building this website, and have plans for a project that uses machine learning to harmonize an input melody.
